📖 How Kudi Arc Send Works
1
Send USDC or EURC from your wallet on Arc Testnet. Gas fees are paid in USDC — near zero cost.
2
Choose recipient country — live exchange rates shown for Nigeria, Kenya, Ghana, Senegal, Cameroon, and more.
3
Enter recipient details — bank account or mobile money number. We support GTBank, OPay, M-Pesa, MTN MoMo, Wave and more.
4
Sign in your wallet — your USDC is approved and the transfer is authorized cryptographically. No passwords needed.
5
Recipient gets cash — NGN, KES, GHS, XOF, FCFA lands in their bank or mobile wallet within minutes on mainnet.
